STRANGE BOAT


Danders Wake is a man who has lost faith in the various fundraising efforts and begins raising money to build a new planet. The book is a literary and comic take on a growing fascination that our culture has for space travel, the space industry, trips to Mars, survival and escapism. The novel deals with the insanity, speed and ferocity of modern life and the desperate attempts that we all may make to get away.


The novel will be published by Rare Bird Books in the spring of 2016.

THIS IS YOUR CAPTAIN SPEAKING


A PILOT, A JOURNALIST, AND A SEMEN TRAFFICKER WALK ONTO A PLANE ... When flight AW2921 crashes into the Hudson River and all 162 passengers survive, it's a Monday morning miracle. What the rapturous public doesn't know: the whole thing was staged as a last-ditch attempt at resurrecting the Air Wanderlust stock price.
