STRANGE BOAT is the story of Danders Wake, a man who has lost faith in the various fundraising efforts and begins raising money to build a new planet. It is a literary and comic take on a growing fascination that our culture has for space travel, the space industry, trips to Mars, survival and escapism. The novel deals with the insanity, speed and ferocity of modern life and the desperate attempts that we all may make to get away.


The novel will be published by Rare Bird Books in the spring of 2016.
